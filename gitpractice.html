Steps to make sandwich:

  1. Butter two slices of bread
  2. Heat up a skillet over medium heat
  3. Layer the bread and cheese onto the skillet
  4. Add some extras, if desired, then the last slice of bread.
  5. Grill the sandwich until it starts to turn a light brown color
  6. Flip the sandwich over and continue grilling it
  7. Serve the sandwich.
